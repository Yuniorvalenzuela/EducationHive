Welcome to Education Hive!

Imagine a vibrant beehive, buzzing with knowledge and energy, where each cell is dedicated to a specific Sustainable Development Goal (SDG). In this space, you'll find everything you need to explore, learn, and play while discovering the impact of each SDG on our world.

Our little friend, Buzzbee, the bee kid, will guide you through each SDG with simple and fun explanations. Buzzbee will bring each story to life, showing you how these goals aim to improve our planet and what we can do to help achieve them.

We also invite you to discover fascinating resources, such as observatories and official NASA data, that will allow you to dive deeper into these topics and connect what you've learned with current science and technology. But that's not all! You'll also get to enjoy interactive games that will test your knowledge and teach you in a fun way.

So, are you ready to join the hive and learn alongside Buzzbee? Let's explore the world of the SDGs together and fly towards a better future!

What are the SDGs?

The Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and improve the lives and prospects of everyone, everywhere. Adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, the 17 SDGs are part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.
